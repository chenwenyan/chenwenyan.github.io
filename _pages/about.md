I am the third-year Ph.D. student majoring in [Computer Science at Cloud and Distributed Systems (CDS) Lab](https://www.fst.um.edu.mo/personal/huanlexu/huanlexu-team/), University of Macau (2021-Present). I am advised by [Huanle Xu](https://www.fst.um.edu.mo/people/huanlexu/) and [Kejiang Ye](https://people.ucas.edu.cn/~kejiang?language=en). My reseach interests focus on Cloud Computing, GPU Multiplexing, Machine Learning System.
